### the big problem

the **entire** project was somehow re-copied into `personal_finance_tracker/`

as a result, there were two sets of `accounts/` apps

there were also some redundant `personal_finance_tracker` "apps" within that directory

### what did I do

- "merged" Layla's accounts app onto the old one
- moved all of the apps into `apps/` instead of keeping them on the base directory
  - why? in order to simplify the base directory and remove possible confusion later on
  - what's different? all external references to an app's must now start with `apps.` e.g. `accounts.models.UserAccount` -> `apps.accounts.models.UserAccount`.
